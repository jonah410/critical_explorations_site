Philosophy of Mind – Introductory

  • Consciousness: An Introduction – Susan Blackmore
    A comprehensive and accessible introduction to the science and philosophy of consciousness.
  • The Mind’s I – Douglas Hofstadter & Daniel Dennett
    A collection of essays and stories exploring self, mind, and consciousness.
  • Philosophy of Mind: A Beginner’s Guide – Ian Ravenscroft
    A clear and concise introduction to the main questions and theories in philosophy of mind.
  • Mind: A Brief Introduction – John Searle
    Searle’s accessible overview of the philosophy of mind, consciousness, and intentionality.
  • Mind: Introduction to Cognitive Science – Paul Thagard
    An approachable introduction to cognitive science and the study of mind.
